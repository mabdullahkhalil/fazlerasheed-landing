## Key Takeaways

1. **No competitor has strong SEO** — The Pakistan MHE market is an SEO blue ocean. First-mover advantage is available.
2. **Content gap is massive** — Almost no competitor publishes educational/buying guide content. Blog strategy will be a major differentiator.
3. **Battery-only positioning is unique** — No competitor specializes exclusively in battery-operated equipment. This is a strong niche to own.
4. **Rental keywords are uncontested** — Most competitors focus on sales; rental-specific SEO is wide open.
5. **Brand comparison content doesn't exist** — "Toyota vs Crown forklift" type content has zero competition in Pakistan market.
6. **Technical SEO is universally poor** — A fast, well-structured Next.js site will outperform all competitors technically.

## Recommended Attack Strategy

### Phase 1: Own the Long-Tail
Target low-competition, high-intent keywords:
- "used [brand] forklift Pakistan"
- "forklift rental Lahore"
- "electric forklift spare parts Pakistan"
- "[brand] forklift battery replacement"

### Phase 2: Contest the Head Terms
Build authority to compete for:
- "forklift for sale in Pakistan"
- "battery operated forklift Pakistan"
- "forklift price in Pakistan"

### Phase 3: Dominate with Content
Create content no competitor has:
- Brand comparison guides
- Buying guides by use case
- Equipment maintenance guides
- Industry-specific guides (warehouse, factory, cold storage)
