## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements Per Post
- **Word count:** 800+ words (buying guides: 1500+)
- **Images:** 3+ original photos or diagrams
- **Internal links:** 3+ to relevant service/equipment/brand pages
- **Schema:** Article/BlogPosting markup
- **Meta:** Unique title (under 60 chars) + description (150–160 chars)
- **Headers:** H1 with keyword, H2/H3 structure
- **CTA:** At least 1 contact/quote CTA per post
