## How to Choose the Right Battery Forklift

### 1. Load Capacity and Load Centre

Start with your heaviest routine load, then add a safety margin. Rated capacity drops as you lift higher or fit attachments, so always check the load chart — a truck rated at 2,000 kg at a 500 mm load centre may handle far less at full mast height.

### 2. Mast Height and Aisle Width

Measure your highest beam level and narrowest aisle. Tight Karachi warehouses often benefit from [reach trucks](/products/reach-trucks) or [stackers](/products/stackers) rather than counterbalance forklifts, which need wider turning space.

### 3. Tyres and Floor Condition

Cushion tyres suit smooth indoor floors; pneumatic tyres handle uneven yards. Worn or unbalanced wheels increase energy use — see our guide to [wheel alignment and balancing](/blog/ultimate-guide-to-forklift-wheel-alignment-and-balancing).

### 4. Battery Chemistry: Lithium-Ion vs Lead-Acid

This is the most important decision for a battery-operated forklift.

| Factor | Lithium-Ion | Lead-Acid |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Upfront cost | Higher | Lower |
| Charge cycles | 2,500–3,000+ | 1,000–1,500 |
| Charging | Opportunity charging, no cooldown | Needs full charge + cooldown |
| Maintenance | Minimal, sealed | Weekly watering, cleaning |
| Best for | 2–3 shift operations | Single-shift operations |
| Hot-climate tolerance | Better with BMS protection | Sensitive to overheating |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which battery-operated forklift is best for port and warehouse work in Karachi?
For high-throughput port and factory operations, a 2,000–3,500 kg lithium-ion counterbalance forklift is ideal — it supports continuous multi-shift work and copes better with coastal humidity when terminals are kept clean and sealed.

### Is a lithium or lead-acid forklift better for Karachi's conditions?
Lithium-ion handles heat and multi-shift use better thanks to its Battery Management System and needs no watering. Lead-acid is cheaper upfront and fine for single-shift work, but requires careful maintenance in Karachi's climate.

### How much does a battery forklift cost in Karachi?
A new battery-operated forklift typically ranges from about PKR 2,200,000 for lighter 1,500–2,000 kg models to PKR 3,200,000–4,800,000 for 5,000 kg-class units, before battery and charger options.
