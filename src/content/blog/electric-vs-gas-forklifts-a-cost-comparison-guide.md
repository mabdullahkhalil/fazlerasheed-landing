# Electric Vs Gas Forklifts: A Cost Comparison Guide

Choosing between electric and gas forklifts in Pakistan can save you money and improve efficiency. Here's what you need to know:

- **Electric Forklifts**: Higher upfront cost (PKR 3,200,000–4,800,000 for 5,000 kg capacity), but cheaper to run long-term with lower energy and maintenance costs. Ideal for indoor use, quieter, and no emissions.
- **Gas Forklifts**: Lower upfront cost (starting at PKR 2,400,000 for 5,000 kg capacity), but higher daily fuel and maintenance expenses. Suitable for outdoor or multi-shift operations with quick refuelling.

## Quick Comparison

| Factor | Electric Forklift | Gas Forklift |
|--------|-------------------|--------------|
| Initial Purchase Cost | PKR 3,200,000–4,800,000 | PKR 2,400,000–4,800,000 |
| Operating Expenses | Lower (electricity costs) | Higher (fuel costs) |
| Maintenance Costs | Lower (fewer parts) | 25–50% higher |
| Service Frequency | Every 500–1,000 hours | Every 250 hours |
| Emissions | None (indoor-friendly) | Produces exhaust fumes |
| Refuelling/Charging Time | Requires charging downtime | Quick refuelling |

**Key Insight**: Electric forklifts are better for long-term savings and indoor use, while gas forklifts suit outdoor and multi-shift operations. Choose based on your budget, work environment, and operational needs.

## 2. Gas Forklift Costs

### Initial Purchase Price

Gas forklifts have a lower upfront cost compared to electric models. However, this price difference should be considered alongside the long-term operational and maintenance costs to get a clearer picture of overall expenses.

### Daily Operating Costs

Running a gas forklift comes with additional costs like fuel consumption and the need for proper fuel storage. These factors, combined with more frequent maintenance, can drive up daily operating expenses.

### Service and Repairs

Gas forklifts require more upkeep due to their combustion systems. Here's what you need to consider:

- **Service Frequency**: Maintenance is needed every 250 hours, compared to every 500 hours for electric forklifts.
- **Higher Costs**: Maintenance expenses are 25–50% more than electric models.
- **Complex Design**: With more moving parts, gas forklifts need regular inspections and part replacements.

### Total Cost Over Time

Although gas forklifts are cheaper to buy, ongoing costs can add up significantly over time:

| Cost Factor | Impact on Total Cost |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Maintenance Intervals | Requires servicing every 250 hours vs 500 hours for electric models |
| Service Costs | 25–50% higher compared to electric forklifts |
| Fuel Expenses | Continuous fuel costs and the need for proper storage |
| Parts Replacement | More frequent due to the complex mechanical system |

Sticking to a regular maintenance schedule is crucial to keep gas forklifts running efficiently and to extend their lifespan.

If you're running a small or medium-sized business with limited funds, gas forklifts are a more affordable option upfront compared to electric models.

Here's how to decide based on your work schedule:

- **Multi-Shift**: Gas forklifts are better for continuous operations. They refuel quickly but may need more frequent maintenance.

### Infrastructure Requirements

Make sure your facility is equipped for the type of forklift you choose:

**For Electric Forklifts:**

- A designated charging area
- Reliable power supply

- Proper ventilation in the charging zone

**For Gas Forklifts:**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the long-term cost advantages of using an electric forklift instead of a gas-powered one?

Electric forklifts provide significant long-term cost advantages over gas-powered forklifts. They are more economical to operate due to lower fuel costs, as electricity is generally more affordable than gas in Pakistan. Additionally, electric forklifts have fewer moving parts, which reduces maintenance expenses and minimises the need for frequent repairs.

Another key benefit is their extended lifespan. With proper care, electric forklifts can last longer, offering better value over time. They are also energy-efficient and environmentally friendly, making them a sustainable choice for businesses aiming to reduce operational costs and environmental impact.

### What are the differences in maintenance requirements between electric and gas forklifts?

Electric forklifts generally require less maintenance compared to gas-powered forklifts. This is because electric forklifts have fewer moving parts and do not rely on components like engines, fuel systems, or exhaust systems, which are common in gas forklifts. Additionally, electric forklifts typically have longer service intervals — around 500 hours — compared to gas forklifts, which usually need servicing every 250 hours.

This reduced maintenance frequency not only lowers upkeep costs but also minimises downtime, leading to improved operational efficiency. Over time, maintenance expenses for electric forklifts can be 25-50% lower than those for gas-powered models, making them a cost-effective option for businesses focused on long-term savings and productivity.

### What should I consider when choosing between an electric or gas forklift for my business in Pakistan?

When deciding between an electric and a gas-powered forklift for your business in Pakistan, consider key factors such as cost, performance, and suitability for your operations.

Electric forklifts are environmentally friendly with zero emissions, making them ideal for indoor use. They require less maintenance due to fewer moving parts and offer long-term savings on operational costs. However, they come with a higher upfront cost, limited runtime due to battery dependency, and require several hours for recharging.

Gas forklifts, on the other hand, are versatile and suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. They provide quick refueling and higher power for heavy-duty tasks. However, they produce emissions, require regular maintenance, and incur higher running costs due to fuel consumption.
